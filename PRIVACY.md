# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

## English

**Collection Miner** (the "Extension") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the Extension.

### 1. Data Processing and Storage
- **Local Only**: All data processed by the Extension is stored and processed **strictly locally** on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browser history, bookmark lists, or preference weights to any external servers.
- **Bookmarks Access**: The Extension requests the `bookmarks` permission to read your bookmarks so it can randomly select and display them on your New Tab page.
- **Storage Access**: The Extension uses the Chrome `storage` API to locally save your bookmark weights, like/dislike counts, and history. This data never leaves your device.
- **Tabs Access**: The Extension requests the `tabs` permission to check for and optionally close duplicate "Collection Miner" tabs, helping to keep your browser tab bar clean.

### 2. Information Sharing
Since we do not collect any of your information, we do not share, sell, or trade any user data with third parties.

### 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in the extension updates and update logs.

## 中文说明

**收藏夹淘金 (Collection Miner)**（以下简称“本插件”）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本插件如何处理您的信息。

### 1. 数据处理与存储
- **完全本地化**：本插件处理的所有数据均**完全保存在您的本地设备上**。我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何个人数据、浏览器历史记录、书签列表或卡片评分权重到任何外部服务器。
- **书签权限 (bookmarks)**：本插件需要读取您的书签，以便在新标签页中随机挑选并展示它们。
- **存储权限 (storage)**：本插件使用 Chrome `storage` API 在本地保存书签展现权重、赞/踩次数和浏览历史。这些数据永远不会离开您的设备。
- **标签页权限 (tabs)**：本插件检测并关闭重复打开的“收藏夹淘金”新标签页，以保持您的标签栏清爽。

### 2. 信息共享
由于我们不收集您的任何信息，因此我们不会向任何第三方共享、出售或交易用户数据。

### 3. 隐私政策的更新
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in the extension updates and update logs.
（我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更新都将通过插件更新及更新日志进行说明。）
