Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-06-25

1. Overview

Collection Miner (the "Extension") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the Extension.

The short version: we collect nothing. All data stays on your device.

2. Data Processing and Storage

  • Local Only: All data processed by the Extension is stored and processed strictly locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browser history, bookmark lists, or preference weights to any external servers.
  • Bookmarks Access (bookmarks permission): The Extension reads your browser bookmarks solely to randomly select and display them on your New Tab page. Bookmark data is never transmitted off-device.
  • Storage Access (storage permission): The Extension uses Chrome's storage API to locally save your bookmark interaction weights, like/dislike counts, and viewing history. This data never leaves your device.
  • Tabs Access (tabs permission): The Extension reads the URL of open tabs to detect and optionally close duplicate "Collection Miner" new tab pages, keeping your browser tab bar clean. No tab content or browsing history is stored or transmitted.

3. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any of your information, we do not share, sell, or trade any user data with third parties.

4. Permissions Summary

  • bookmarks — Read-only access to display your bookmarks. Never modified or transmitted.
  • storage — Local storage of interaction weights and history. Never synced or transmitted.
  • tabs — Reads current tab URLs to close duplicates only. No browsing history stored.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in the extension version updates and this page. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on GitHub.

1. 概述

收藏夹淘金 (Collection Miner)（以下简称"本插件"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本插件如何处理您的信息。

简而言之：我们不收集任何数据，所有数据均保留在您的本地设备上。

2. 数据处理与存储

  • 完全本地化：本插件处理的所有数据均完全保存在您的本地设备上。我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何个人数据、浏览器历史记录、书签列表或卡片评分权重到任何外部服务器。
  • 书签权限（bookmarks）：本插件仅读取您的书签，以便在新标签页中随机挑选并展示它们。书签数据绝不会传输到设备之外。
  • 存储权限（storage）：本插件使用 Chrome storage API 在本地保存书签展现权重、赞/踩次数和浏览历史。这些数据永远不会离开您的设备。
  • 标签页权限（tabs）：本插件读取当前打开标签页的 URL，以检测并关闭重复打开的"收藏夹淘金"新标签页，保持您的标签栏清爽。不会存储或传输任何标签内容或浏览历史。

3. 信息共享

由于我们不收集您的任何信息，因此我们不会向任何第三方共享、出售或交易用户数据。

4. 权限说明

  • bookmarks — 只读访问，用于展示书签，不会修改或传输。
  • storage — 本地存储交互权重和历史，不会同步或传输。
  • tabs — 读取当前标签页 URL 以关闭重复标签，不存储浏览历史。

5. 隐私政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更新都将通过插件版本更新及本页面进行说明。继续使用本插件即表示接受更新后的政策。

6. 联系方式

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请在 GitHub 上提交 Issue。
